Programme

Opening
Puja & Aarti
By: Suparna Basu · Swaratmika group (Carnatic)
Traditional devotional start to the evening.
Ganesh Stuti (Kathak)
Solo performance by: Paloma Sen
A talented senior student of MDA. Paloma completed her Bharatanatyam Arangetram last year – tonight she brings her artistry in Kathak with a graceful invocation to Lord Ganesha.
Slokam (Bharatanatyam)
Solo performance by: Lopa Mistry
Lopa, a talented senior student of MDA who completed her Bharatanatyam Arangetram last year, presents a devotional Bharatanatyam performance to a Slokam.
I. Vaishakha
Abhinaya by - Asha Fernando
Dance 1: Bengali Poila Boishakh Dance
Performed by: Ruheli Chatterji and Purba Chatterji
Description: A vibrant traditional Bengali dance celebrating the Bengali New Year.
Dance 2: Dasavatar
Performed by: Samiksha Singh, Hritvi Joshi, and Vrusha Mistry
Description: A Bharatanatyam classical portrayal of the ten divine incarnations of Lord Vishnu.
II. Jyeshtha
Abhinaya by - Hritvi Joshi
Dance 3: Shiva – Maheshwari Community Presentation
Performed by: Asha Fernando, Hannah Sandhu, Shiva Ranam, Harshaa Prasad, Prerna Barcan and Angel Rose.
Description: A Kathak dance devotional offering to Lord Shiva, representing cosmic energy and transformation.
Dance 4: Vat Savitri
Performed by: Navya Gupta, Ravvee Acharya, Navisha Naran, Meera Bhanusgali, Divya Balram, Asha Balram, Isabel Arun, Nylah Arun, Tia Siva, Aradhya Bhatnagar, Sameera Wessel, Saarthi Tataria, Mishika Bist, Yana Bhatt, Samira Naidu, Krishi Patel, Kavisha Srivastava, Danika Pallavi, Annvita Sae Cheerla, Tanusha Malhotra, Purnatejita Cheerla
Description: A narrative of Savitri’s devotion and bravery, honoring the spirit of womanhood.
III. Ashadha
Abhinaya by - Samiksha Singh
Dance 5: Jaganath Ratha Yatra
Performed by: Yuthu Dineshkumar, Gokila Mylsamy, Sharanne, Saranya Lakshmanasamy,
Description: A vibrant presentation inspired by Lord Jagannath’s Ratha Yatra, showcasing the festive spirit and divine procession.
Dance 6: Guru Purnima Dance
Performed by: Navya Gupta, Ravvee Acharya, Navisha Naran, Meera Bhanusgali, Divya Balram, Asha Balram, Isabel Arun, Nylah Arun, Aradhya Bhatnagar, Sameera Wessel, Saarthi Tataria, Yana Bhatt, Samira Naidu, Krishi Patel, Annvita Sae Cheerla, Tanusha Malhotra,
Description: A graceful tribute to the Gurus, celebrating the spiritual lineage and honoring teachers through expressive movement.
Dance 7: Jaganath Ratha Yatra (Kathak)
Performed by: Prerna Barcan, Shiva Ranam, Harshaa Prasad, Angel Rose
Description: A dynamic Kathak performance capturing the grandeur and devotion of the Ratha Yatra festival.
IV. Shravana
Abhinaya by - Vrusha Mistry
Dance 8: Teej
Performed by: Madhumita Choudhury, Monica Jose, and Uma Nair
Description: A joyful and expressive dance celebrating the Teej festival, symbolizing monsoon romance and the devotion of Parvati to Lord Shiva.
V. Bhadrapada
Abhinaya by - Harshaa Prasad
Dance 9: Krishna Leela Ganapati Vandana
Performed by: Uma Nair, Keerthi Midhin, Krishna Midhin, Dahiri Smith, Aahana Gupta
Description: A captivating dance drama narrating playful and divine episodes from the life of Lord Krishna, filled with grace, devotion, and expression.
Dance 10: Ganapati Vandana
Performed by: Danika Pallavi, Purnatejita Cheerla, Srujana Pannasi, Neha Malhotra, Mishika Bist, Bhavishya Medarametta, Varuni Parmar, Kavisha Srivastava, Tia Siva
Description: A traditional Bharatanatyam invocation dance honoring Lord Ganesha, seeking blessings for wisdom and success at the beginning of auspicious events.
Dance 11: Onam Celebration
Performed by: Surani Gunawardana, Anita Tataria, Charu Khera, Anita Bist, Sree Lakshmi, Deepa Boja, Deepa Madhavan, Deepa Shankar, Saranya Lakshmanasamy, Raman Kaur
Description: A colourful and rhythmic performance showcasing the joy, cultural richness, and traditions of Kerala’s harvest festival, Onam.
VI. Ashvin
Abhinaya by - Hritvi Joshi
Dance 12: Devi Vandana – Navaratri Special
Performed by: Madhumita Choudhury, Monica Jose, Shanaya Bhatiya, Keerthi Midhin, Krishna Midhin, Krisha Patel
Description: A powerful Kathak invocation to the Divine Mother, celebrating the nine forms of Durga during Navaratri with rhythmic footwork and graceful expressions.
Dance 13: Goddess Lakshmi Dance
Performed by: Samiksha Singh, Shivkhya Singh, Ruheli Chatterji, Vrusha Mistry
Description: A devotional Bharatanatyam performance dedicated to Goddess Lakshmi, symbolizing prosperity, abundance, and divine grace.
Dance 14: Krishna Leela
Performed by: Yuthu Dineshkumar, Mihika Buddha, Aria Pathania, Anaina Kanmatha Reddy, Meera Ramesh
Description: A sweet and joyful dance by our youngest performers, portraying stories from Lord Krishna’s early life with innocence and charm.
VII. Kartika
Abhinaya by - Vrusha Mistry
Dance 15: Kali – The Fierce Mother
Performed by: Samiksha Singh, Shivkhya Singh, Ruheli Chatterji, Vrusha Mistry, Neha Kollati, Shreeya Vishnu, Tanvi Kurupur
Description: A Bharatanatyam powerful and intense portrayal of Goddess Kali, the embodiment of time and transformation, capturing her fierce yet protective energy.
Dance 16: Lakshmi – Light and Prosperity
Performed by: Sivathmika, Shenaya Handattu, Aahana Gupta, Keerthi Midhin, Krishna Midhin, Dahiri Smith
Description: A Bharatanatyam serene and graceful dance in honor of Goddess Lakshmi, marking the Diwali celebration and invoking blessings of wealth and well-being.
Dance 17: Madhurashtakam
Performed by: Hannah Sandhu, Asha Fernando, Hritvi Joshi
Description: A devotional Bharatanatyam and Kathak jugalbandhi piece expressing the divine sweetness of Lord Krishna, composed by Vallabhacharya. Each movement reflects love, bhakti, and poetic beauty.
Dance 18: Karva Chauth
Performed by: Tripti Gupta, Anita Tataria, Charu Khera, Anita Bist
Description: A heartfelt and expressive dance celebrating the Karva Chauth festival, showcasing the strength and devotion of women in traditional rituals.
VIII. Magha
Abhinaya by - Samiksha Singh
Dance 19: Saraswati Vandana
Performed by: Shiva Ranam, Ninu Ann, Hritvi Joshi, Raksha Nand, Dwiti Joshi
Description: A Bharatanatyam graceful and serene invocation to Goddess Saraswati, the embodiment of knowledge, wisdom, and the arts. This dance honors the divine source of learning and creativity during the auspicious month of Magh.
IX. Phalguna
Abhinaya by - Eva Gupta
Dance 20: Shiva Shruti
Performed by: Siddhi Natraj, Pakhi Karwal, Anita Bist, Trisha Dhillon, Tripti Gupta, Charu Khera, Myra Sharma, Subhdra Singh, Seeta Chandra, Anita Tataria
Description: A Kathak majestic and spiritually uplifting performance devoted to Lord Shiva, celebrating the cosmic dance of creation and dissolution. The dance reflects the divine rhythm and power associated with the festival of Maha Shivaratri.
X. Chaitra
Abhinaya by - Vinita Gupta
Dance 21: Ram Navami
Performed by: Uma Nair, Zara Subrayan, Midhin Gopan
Description: A devotional Bharatanatyam dance celebrating the birth of Lord Rama, embodying his virtues of righteousness, courage, and compassion.
Dance 22: Gudi Padwa (Gudi Parva)
Performed by: Tanvi Kurupur, Shreeya Vishnu, Neha Kollati
Description: A vibrant and traditional Maharashtrian celebration to mark the New Year, signifying joy, renewal, and prosperity.
Dance 23: Ugadi
Performed by: Siddhi Natraj, Pakhi Karwal, Trisha Dhillon, Myra Sharma, Subhdra Singh, Seeta Chandra
Description: A colourful celebration of the Telugu and Kannada New Year, expressing hopes for a fruitful year ahead through classical movement and festive flair.
Dance 24: Hanuman Chalisa
Performed by: Shiva Ranam, Ninu Ann, Hritvi Joshi, Raksha Nand, Dwiti Joshi, Uma Nair, Zara Subrayan, Midhin Gopan
Description: A Bharatanatyam powerful and energetic group performance invoking the strength and devotion of Lord Hanuman through the sacred verses of the Hanuman Chalisa.
Dance 25: Holi – Grand Finale
Performed by: Entire Mayur Dance Academy Ensemble
Description: A joyful, colourful, and high-energy celebration of Holi, symbolizing unity, love, and the triumph of good over evil. This dance brings together every performer in a spirited finale to close the production with festive cheer.
